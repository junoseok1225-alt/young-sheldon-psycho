Who is Young Sheldon?

Young Sheldon Cooper, a child prodigy from the show Young Sheldon, displays fascinating behaviour shaped by his environment, upbringing, and personality. This project analyses his behaviour using five key psychological approaches.

Cognitive Approach

The cognitive approach focuses on mental processes such as thinking, memory, and problem-solving. In Young Sheldon, we can see how Sheldon's incredible memory, logical thinking, and attention to detail influence his behaviour and interactions. This approach helps explain why Sheldon often relies on reasoning rather than emotions to navigate social situations.
